Here's What I Do

#1. Freelance Portfolio

I have worked with a number of clients on a freelance basis, these clients have included Ted Baker, XPO Logistics, DouNutz and more!

DouNutz

A doughnut company commissioned me to create a detailed marketing strategy, new branding and manage their social media channels. I recommended they keep the branding and social media content playful to engage with their audience.

To increase engagement on social media, I released a series of teasers ahead of going live with the new branding. I created and designed the artwork and copywriting on all social media channels, increasing post engagement by 666% and post reach by 122%.

The logo was a success with the client and the feedback from their customers was that they loved it.

Spotlight Cleaning

Spotlight Cleaning commissioned me to create a business flyer, including designing their branding. The brief was to keep the branding feminine.

Once the business flyer landed on social media, bookings increased by 70%.

#2. Roxanne Jayne Photography

Having a love for photography and being business minded, I set up my own photography business.

When designing the logo, I took into consideration the demographics of customers, their personas and colour theory.

When creating the gift cards, I used images of the target audience and purposely left a blank space so that the customer could personalise the gift card.

Two of my most successful campaigns on social media reached 20,195 and 29,384 organically.

‘Model call outs’ and giveaways were popular with customers, they required the customer to comment, like and share which increased the reach. I took ROI into account, the return on bookings due to the social media coverage outweighed the costs involved.

#3. Ann Summers

During my time working as an Ann Summers Rep, I ran a number of marketing campaigns.

Thinking outside of the box but also keeping in mind of ROI, I put together a promotional pack for new customers. I attached a personalised sticker to the catalogue; this included a photo of me. I wanted to stand out from the competition as being unique and personal. Also included, were low-cost freebies to give an incentive to these new customers.

I kept to the Ann Summers branding when creating business cards (pink, white and black). My contact number is present, giving the customer a call to action.

Once my business cards arrived, I posted a photo on social media and on the Ann Summers Rep page. This increased engagement on social media by 150%. The Ann Summers Reps asked where I made the business cards and that it was such a good idea. I negotiated a referral scheme with the company who I purchased the business cards from and got £10 for each new customer that I referred.

Utilising my skill set in forming 3rd party b2b relationships, I contacted Firefly Photography and agreed joint business development. They provided me with a complimentary photo shoot including images, usually worth 1k. I used these images to showcase the Ann Summers lingerie by modelling in it. I then posted these images on social media. This increased sales and engagement by 200%. I also referred lots of customers to Firefly for photoshoots of their own.
